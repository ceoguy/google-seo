# Preventing and monitoring abuse on your site

 
 
 The following topics describe how you can prevent and monitor abuse on your site.

 Spammers often take advantage of open comment forms and other user generated content
 inputs and generate spammy content on an unsuspecting victim site. Learn how to prevent
 and monitor abuse on your site or platform. |
